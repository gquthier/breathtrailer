FAQ

What is the lead time? +

Our typical lead time is 3-4 months depending on the model and customization selected. We will keep you updated throughout the process to ensure you're informed every step of the way.

Can I customize my Breath Trailer? +

Absolutely! The Breath Trailer is fully customizable. If you have specific requests for features like a TV or Bluetooth speakers that are not included in the 2D builder, just let us know, and we can definitely accommodate your needs.

What vehicle do you recommend for towing a Breath Trailer? +

The Breath Trailer is lightweight (starting at 720kg) and can be towed by most standard vehicles, such as mid-sized SUVs and trucks. If you're unsure about your vehicle's towing capacity, feel free to reach out, and well help you check if its suitable.

Does the Breath Trailer offer any solutions for bringing bikes along? +

Yes! The Breath Trailer is equipped to carry bikes. It includes a 2 hitch receiver at the rear, rated for 200lbs, which is ideal for adding a bike rack.

Is the entire cabin insulated? +

Yes, the Breath Trailer's cabin is fully insulated. The insulation helps maintain comfort inside the trailer in a range of weather conditions, making it ideal for both hot and cold climates.

Do you offer solar power options? +

Yes! The Breath Trailer comes with the option to add solar power capabilities. You can equip your trailer with solar panels and a charge controller, allowing you to generate renewable energy while you enjoy the great outdoors.

What is the maximum tire size? +

The maximum tire size the Breath Trailer supports is 30 inches. With the off-road upgrade, you can select more rugged tires for challenging terrains.

Can I stand up inside a Breath Trailer? +

No, the Breath Trailers interior height is 1200mm, designed for sitting and sleeping comfort. Its compact design prioritizes mobility, ease of towing, and efficient use of space.

Do you offer financing options? +

Yes, we offer financing options for the Breath Trailer! Please contact us for more details on available financing options and how to apply.
